Continual ImprovementOngoing improvement across products, services and practices.

Purpose

  • Improve value, performance and outcomes.
  • Applies at all organisational levels.

Model

  • What is the vision?
  • Where are we now?
  • Where do we want to be?
  • How do we get there?
  • Take action and evaluate results.

Register

  • Capture improvement ideas.
  • Track opportunities and actions.
  • Prioritise based on value.

Measurement

  • Use baselines and targets.
  • Measure before and after changes.
  • Use evidence to confirm improvement.
Validation note: Use this as a learning aid and as a first validation layer for related practice questions.
