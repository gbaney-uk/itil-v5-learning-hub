The Four Dimensions

This is a starter mind-map layout. Replace the wording with your actual ITIL V5 Foundation notes.

The Four Dimensions

Organisations and People

Roles, responsibilities, culture, communication and skills.

Information and Technology

Information, knowledge, tools, systems and security.

Partners and Suppliers

External organisations, supplier relationships and contracts.

Value Streams and Processes

Activities, workflows, procedures and how value is created.

Exam Takeaway

The four dimensions help make sure service management is considered from all key angles, not just technology.

Practice Questions

Question 1

Why should service management consider all four dimensions?

Because focusing on only one area, such as technology, can create weak services. Good service management needs people, technology, suppliers, processes and value streams to work together.
